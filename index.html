    • Passionate to create, crazy explorer and hungry to learn

    I am a multi-disciplinary designer specializing in UX design and graphic design at GALE Partners and on the side, I am learning Front-end development at Hackeryou. I believe using design-thinking methods, technology and foresight in hopes to enhance the human needs and solutions of the future I love and have an appreciation for all things creative. By using design to solve problems, I get to help make people's lives easier and that gives me great satisfaction. My strong sense of curiosity pushes me to grow, seek challenges, and keeps me wanting to learn more. I strongly believe that empathy, curiosity, and storytelling are skills that all designers should have. I'm passionate about developing my leadership skills, self-discovery journey, and well-being. I hope to inspire others as others have done for me.
